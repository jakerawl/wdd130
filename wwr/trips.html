Offered Trips!

So you have decided to go on an adventure, or maybe you haven't yet. Either way you need to know what kind of adventure you are ready for. Below we will go over our top three river trips that we do. Each trip will be labeled with a difficulty level, there will be easy, medium, and hard. Read on and decide which one is for you! Remember if you have any questions feel free to Contact Us!

Our Top Three River Trips!

The first and number one mist popular river trip is down the Dark River. This river is for beginners or anyone that likes a calm trip down the river. The banks of the Dark River offer some truly magnificent foliage and a diverse wildlife population. Form deer to bear you will feel truly immersed in nature. The Dark river offers 3 different stretches of rapids the most intense being a class 2, just enough to splash some water into the boat.

The Blue River offers a great adventure for those rafters coming for a second time or those that want a little more adventure. Much like the Dark River the blue river offers a variety of sights that truly immerses you in nature. Unlike the Dark River the Blue River has rapids that are a little more intense. The highest rapid class being a class 3. Don't let that scare you away, a lot of people say the Blue River is one of their favorites.

The full day trip is truly for the most extreme adventurer. It follows the Blue River but instead of getting off halfway through to avoid those big rapids we go right through them! You can expect to be on the river all day riding through some of the most famous and intense rapids. This trip is recommended to those that have experience with rapids or those that truly want a full experience.
